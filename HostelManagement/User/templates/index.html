Hogwarts

The Hogwarts Mens Hostel, established in 2005 , is located within the College campus and is an integral part of the College. Hogwarts Mens’ Hostel aims to provide convenient and comfortable stay for the outstation male students.

About us

Governance of Hogwarts

The hostel affairs are co-ordinated under the guidance and supervision of the resident Warden. The warden is assisted by hostel students’ union. The hostel committee under the chairmanship of the Principal, the college management frames the rules and regulations of the hostel and is the final authority in matters concerning the hostel.
